Route C entry statement and frontier completeness, no characteristic-`3`
Picard-rank-one klt del Pezzo surface has `>= 8` singular points.

descendant with elliptic boundary.

Pezzo surface with `>= 8` singularities and a positive root-contractible
contact-`2` configuration, either the configuration already gives a descendant

bridge germs beyond the first analyzed bridge frontier.

Corollary `9.82` then says that bridge completeness cannot be purely local and
must use an additional global restriction.
