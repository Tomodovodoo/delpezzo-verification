## Normalized Claim

Lemma `9.83` gives the standard ruling construction from a smooth rational
`0`-curve.

Lemma `9.84` computes the arithmetic genus created when a smooth rational curve
meets a contracted `(-1)`-curve with multiplicity `m`.

Proposition `9.85` treats a broad one-component bridge family
`[b] ⊣ [a,(2)^(a-1)]`, claiming an image-square formula, root-contractibility,
height bounds after partial contraction, and an elliptic-boundary descendant
when `a = 3`.

Corollary `9.86` claims an anticanonical bound
`-K_X · C = -1 + 2/b + (2a - 1)/(a^2 - a + 1)` for that family and derives the
constraints `b <= 3` when `a >= 5` and `b <= 4` when `a = 4`.

Corollary `9.87` then claims that no positive root-contractible bridge of that
family can occur on a benchmark counterexample.

## Agreed Findings

- Lemma `9.83` verifies. The cohomology sequence gives `h^0 = 2`, the
  restriction map shows no base point on `F`, the member `F` itself excludes
  base points off `F`, and the base-point-free pencil defines a morphism to
  `P^1`.
- Lemma `9.84` verifies. The pullback decomposition `phi^*T = U + mE`,
  the computations of `T^2` and `K_Y · T`, and the adjunction formula all match
  and give `p_a(T) = m(m - 1)/2`.
- Proposition `9.85` is locally plausible but not fully checkable from this
  packet alone. The determinant computation in part `(1)` and the local peeling
  induction in parts `(2)` and `(3)` are numerically consistent, but the proof
  relies on external/global notions such as `D`, “rest boundary,” `ht(X)`,
  “descendant,” and the disjointness of all remaining boundary components.
- Corollary `9.86` has a real written derivation error. All three primaries
  flagged the false displayed equalities
  `1 + 1/b = 2/b` and
  `a + (a - 1)/(a^2 - a + 1) = (2a - 1)/(a^2 - a + 1)`.
  So even if the intended final formula may be recoverable from Proposition
  `9.47`, the proof as written does not derive it correctly.
- Corollary `9.87` was split `unclear / unclear / gap` at the primary level.
  The `unsure` pair agreed that it should be upgraded to a real `gap`, because
  it relies first on already-gapped Corollary `9.86` and then delegates every
  actual exclusion step to out-of-scope Corollary `9.88(i)-(iii)`.
